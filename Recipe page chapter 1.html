Filipino Chicken Adobo

The Chicken Adobo is a classic Filipino dish known for its savory, saucy, and slightly sweet and spicy flavors.
It is a simple yet delicious dish made with chicken and you can also use pork or duck with soy sauce, vinegar, and aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of Chicken
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper powder
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 tablespoons fish sauce

Cooking Method

  1. The pan receives medium heat oil before adding garlic and onions to cook until they develop a pleasant aroma.
  2. Add the chicken pieces, black pepper powder, fish sauce and cook until lightly browned.
  3. Pour in the soy sauce, cover it until the chicken absorb the soy sauce.
  4. Add red chilli and sugar (if using).
  5. Add the vinegar, cover and simmer for about 30 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  6. Uncover, make it to low hear and let the sauce reduce until thickened to your desired consistency.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy!
